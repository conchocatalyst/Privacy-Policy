Privacy Policy

App: Catalyst Stock Tracker
Last updated: February 26, 2026

Overview

Catalyst Stock Tracker is a personal finance app that allows you to track stocks, manage a watchlist, and view earnings calendars. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data is accessed and how it is used.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. We do not require account registration, and no user data is transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Data Sources

The app fetches publicly available stock market data from the following third-party APIs:

  • Finnhub – for real-time stock quotes and financial data
  • Yahoo Finance – for additional market data

These services have their own privacy policies. Your use of this app is subject to their respective terms.

Photo Library Access

The app requests access to your photo library solely to allow you to import watchlist screenshots. Photos are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

Data Storage

All app data (watchlists, portfolio entries, settings) is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers by us.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@conchocatalyst.com
